How SmartQ Works?

  • Users simply scan a QR code on their mobile devices to join the queue.
  • A webpage will open displaying the required fields (Name, Mobile Number) for the student to fill out and submit.
  • After submission, they can view their queue number, track how many are ahead, and monitor the queue in real time.
  • They will receive a reminder notification once they are next in line to be served.
  • Service is delivered safely and comfortably.
